Visit Our Farm


Sunshine Farm is 110 acre Certified Organic family farm in East Greenwich, We welcome visitors at our farm and at local farm and at local farmers markets. Get to know your farmer, get to know your food. During your visit, you and your children may witness animals reproducing, birthing, nursing young, or dying. We encourage you to use your visit an opportunity to talk openly about life cycles, and that farm animals are raised for food.
