About

Stacey grew up on a farm in Massachusetts She wants to showcase locally grown food at her restaurant. She is interested in promoting food sustainability Baby steps and leaps of faith brought Stacey to where she is today. A yearning for culinary training later led Stacey explore local farms and using those ingredients as farm to table Stacy believes in supporting local farms and is actively involved in promoting local farmers through events and other activities. Her story is one of guts and determination to follow her dreams of being her own boss and sharing her love of people and cuisine.
